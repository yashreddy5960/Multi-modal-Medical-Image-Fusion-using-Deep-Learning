About Medical Image Fusion

Medical image fusion combines complementary information from multiple imaging modalities into a single composite image that is more informative and suitable for visual perception and computer-assisted diagnostic methods.

Our platform supports fusion of:

  • MRI + CT: Combines soft tissue contrast with bone structure
  • CT + PET: Merges anatomical detail with metabolic activity
  • MRI + PET: Integrates soft tissue information with functional data
